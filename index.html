Tension Is Growing: Cyberpunk 2077 Release Date Is Postponed! What To Do?!

Jul 09, 2024

The approximate release date is pushed back to September 2020, and we can only speculate why: some blame the developers, some suspect financial reasons. There are rumors that even coronavirus could have been the cause of our suffering. Anyway, what are we going to do while CD Projekt RED works out the kinks of Cyberpunk 2077?

Being the most anticipated game of the year, it has already raised a fuss over its elaborate graphics and celebs starring in it. Right from the moment of the official announcement, the team kept us up-to-date and gave a little hint on what awaits us in the game almost every week. Even after the release delay, the studio keeps providing us with some delicious details of the creative process.

We've already seen the BREATHTAKING trailer starring Keanu Reeves, but not many of us know that CD Project RED released a new video with the detailed testing of Cyberpunk gameplay.

What are the latest updates on Cyberpunk 2077?

  • Xbox One X players will get a free upgrade to the Series X version by the time of its release;
  • musician Grimes revealed spoiler-filled details on Lizzy Wizzy, the character she's playing;
  • the game will be compatible with GeForce Now and Google Stadia;
  • the monetization for Cyberpunk 2077's multiplayer is still not ruled out;
  • there will be a "hardcore mode" which will turn off the UI;
  • one of the game creators will cameo in the game.
